IT'S GO TIME! WELCOME TO धरोहर 2024!

Dharohar is an exciting three-day festival at ABES Engineering College, celebrating culture, creativity, and innovation.

The festival features thrilling attractions like a Ferris wheel and roller coaster, alongside interactive games.

At its core, Dharohar showcases vibrant cultural performances, including dance, music, and DJ acts.
